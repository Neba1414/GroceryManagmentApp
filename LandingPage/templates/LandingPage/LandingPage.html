What do we do?

Covid-19 will likely have a permanent effects on the way we work and interact
in busy places such as the grocery store. The idea for this app was born in an
effort to reduce exposure by helping people find what they need quickly rather
than spending a lot of time going through the shopping store.

How Does it Work?

    1. Create an account and register

    2. Search and choose your item to buy

    3. Place your order and add a location for delivery
